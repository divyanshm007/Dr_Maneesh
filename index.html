About the Doctor

A physician who treats the whole person, not just the report.

Dr. Maneesh Kr. Srivastava is a Senior Consultant Physician and Lifestyle Disease Expert practising in Lucknow. He completed his MBBS from Muzaffarnagar Medical College (Ch. Charan Singh University, Meerut) and his MD in Internal Medicine from Seth G.S. Medical College, Parel, Mumbai — graduating as a Gold Medalist.

He has served at K.G.M.U. Vivekanand Polyclinic, SIPS Hospital and SPPGIMS, and is trained in advanced life support by the International Training Centre at King George's Medical University, the American Heart Association and the American College of Surgeons. Today he leads Deema Medicare and the Deemax Hospital & Institute of Integrated Medicine and Sciences — a preventive and curative practice built around long-term relationships with families.

Mission

Make specialist-grade internal medicine accessible, unhurried and affordable for every family in Lucknow.

Vision

Shift care from crisis to prevention — catching lifestyle disease years before it becomes an emergency.
